WONDER POEM

I wonder what to wonder
For instance, do frogs have to wait
An hour after they soothe their hunger
before they go under?

However, my favorite one of all
Could perhaps be a downfall
It’s a funny one I must say
but it might ruin someone’s getaway

How do you know
When your invisible ink is out?
Letters and letters sent
unintentionally blank

Imagine, secret spies
with vital information
forgot to be wise
when reporting to the nation
